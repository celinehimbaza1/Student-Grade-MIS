Student Grades Management System

A Student Grade Management System is a software application designed to help educational institutions efficiently manage and track students' academic performance. The system enables educators to input, store, and retrieve student grades for various subjects and assignments. It offers features like grade calculation, performance analysis, and reporting, allowing teachers to generate transcripts or performance reports quickly.
